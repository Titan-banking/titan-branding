### Usage Rules

- **Wordmark:** `titan` in lowercase Merriweather Bold serif
- **Preferred lockup:** Mark left of wordmark, horizontal orientation
- **On dark surfaces:** Mark in Copper Orange, wordmark in Ivory White
- **On light surfaces:** Mark in Copper Orange, wordmark in Charcoal Slate or Evergreen Slate
- **Clear space:** Maintain padding equal to the cap-height of the wordmark on all sides
- **Do not:** recolor the mark to anything outside the brand palette, stretch the lockup, or place on low-contrast backgrounds

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **Archetype** | Sage / Creator |
| **Core Feeling** | Trusted intelligence — like a knowledgeable partner, not a tool |
| **Adjectives** | Elevated, precise, thoughtful, approachable |
| **Avoid** | Casual, jargon-heavy, hype-driven, overly technical without purpose |

### Writing Principles

1. **Lead with clarity.** Say the most important thing first.
2. **Be precise, not verbose.** Fewer words at higher quality.
3. **Earn trust through specificity.** Avoid vague claims; back statements with evidence.
4. **Maintain warmth.** Elevated does not mean cold — Titan is a partner, not a platform.

## Application Guidelines

### Documents & Reports
- Primary surfaces: Evergreen Slate + Ivory White
- Accent / highlight elements: Copper Orange (sparingly — headlines, callout borders, icons)
- Dividers: Warm Sand or Dusty Sage
- Typography: Charcoal Slate for body, Ash Gray for captions

### Presentations (PPTX)
- Title slides: Evergreen Slate background, Ivory White type, Copper Orange accent mark
- Content slides: Ivory White background, Charcoal Slate type
- Section dividers: Mid Green or Muted Teal backgrounds
- Charts/data: Use brand palette — avoid default Office colors

### UI / Product
- Navigation / sidebar: Mid Green `#243D30` or Evergreen Slate
- CTAs / buttons: Copper Orange `#FF6E3C`
- Input borders / dividers: Warm Sand `#D7CFC7`
- Success / active states: Dusty Sage `#A7B9A7` or Muted Teal `#3D5C52`
